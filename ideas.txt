4: grabbed ball, turn
Hardcoded.

5: near end
Ultrasonic on, Following line.

6: Closing in on Line

7: found second box, drop ball
Simply dropping ball. Waiting.

8: finished.
Done. Standstill.

9: Something unexpected happen.
Propagate to the other and stop doing anything at all.
